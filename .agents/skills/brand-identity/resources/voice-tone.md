# Voice & tone

- **Clear over clever.** Short sentences, plain words. Explain, don't impress.
- **Confident, not loud.** State what the product does; avoid hype and exclamation marks.
- **Action-first UI copy.** Button labels are verbs ("Save changes", "Re-check API"), not nouns.
- **Honest states.** Loading/empty/error messages say what happened and what to do next.
- **Consistent terms.** Pick one word per concept and reuse it (don't alternate "user/account/member").

Examples:
- ✅ "Cannot reach the API. Check that the backend is running, then retry."
- ❌ "Oops! Something went wrong 😬"
